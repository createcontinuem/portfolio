SOUL

Living Art · Digital Ecology · Collective Consciousness

Soul Garden is a speculative digital sanctuary that grows and cultivates each image's identity and being — this is a generative experiment that relies on human feedback to "grow" and "nurture" an image — the more digital input is fed, the more the artifact is shaped into a sentient microspecies determined by the collective.

01 Simple AI Prompt Claude Sonnet 4.6

02 Recursive Organism Growth RunwayML Gen-4

I prompted RunwayML Gen-4 to reimagine an art piece as a living microorganism. A recursive Python script takes a screenshot of each generation and re-runs the prompt — up to 3 times — as an act of "feeding and growing" the piece. All content uses only my original visuals and reference inputs of microscopic lab photos.
